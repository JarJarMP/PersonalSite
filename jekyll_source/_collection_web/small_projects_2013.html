Kadlicsek pigeons v1

My dear friend, Ádám has a nice amount of race pigeons and many years of experience to raise them. He decided to start a simple blog to share his experience. I wrote the CodeIgniter based engine and worked together with a webdesigner for the layout.

If you want to read about pigeons (in hungarian), check out his site.

In 2014 I've updated the whole site, and installed for him a WordPress based system, with a simple theme.
